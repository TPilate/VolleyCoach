### Drill Entry 17: 2-v-2 Cross-Court
* **Title:** 2-v-2 Cross-Court Scrimmage
* **Category:** Gameplay
* **Tags:** ["Small Sided", "Ball Control", "Communication", "Warm-up"]
* **durationMinutes:** 15
* **minPlayers:** 4
* **maxPlayers:** 12
* **Purpose:** * Maximize touches for every player in a competitive environment.
    * Force players to read the attacker and cover lateral court space.
    * Improve out-of-system communication.
* **Instructions:**
    1. Divide the court in half longitudinally (down the middle). Players can only use one half of the court (e.g., Zone 1, 2, and half of 6).
    2. Put 2 players on each side of the net within that half-court.
    3. Coach initiates with a free ball. Players must use 3 touches (Pass, Set, Hit).
    4. Play standard rally scoring to 7 points, then rotate new teams in or switch cross-court angles.
* **sourceManual:** "VolleyCoach Core Library"

### Drill Entry 18: Middle Blocker Transition & Quick Strike
* **Title:** Middle Blocker Transition & 1-Ball
* **Category:** Attack
* **Tags:** ["Middles", "Transition", "Quick Attack", "Footwork"]
* **durationMinutes:** 15
* **minPlayers:** 3
* **maxPlayers:** 8
* **Purpose:** * Train Middle Blockers to transition aggressively off the net after a block to make themselves available for a quick attack.
    * Improve setter-middle timing on the "1-ball" (quick set).
* **Instructions:**
    1. Middle Blocker (MB) starts at the net in a blocking posture. Setter is in position at the net. Coach is in the back row with balls.
    2. MB executes a block jump. 
    3. Upon landing, MB must quickly transition off the net (opening to the court) behind the 3-meter line.
    4. As the MB transitions, the Coach tosses a perfect pass to the Setter.
    5. The MB approaches immediately for a quick "1-ball" attack. 
    6. Run 10 consecutive reps per Middle Blocker to build stamina.
* **sourceManual:** "VolleyCoach Core Library"

### Drill Entry 19: Serving Around the World
* **Title:** Serving Around the World
* **Category:** Serve/Receive
* **Tags:** ["Serving", "Accuracy", "Focus"]
* **durationMinutes:** 10
* **minPlayers:** 2
* **maxPlayers:** 14
* **Purpose:** * Develop pinpoint serving accuracy across all 6 defensive zones.
    * Train servers to adjust their power and trajectory on command.
* **Instructions:**
    1. Place cones or visual markers in the center of all 6 zones on the receiving side (1, 6, 5, 4, 3, 2).
    2. Every player grabs a ball and lines up behind the serving line.
    3. Players must serve and hit (or land within 3 feet of) Zone 1. 
    4. Once a player hits Zone 1, they "unlock" Zone 2 for their next serve. 
    5. Players race to successfully serve all 6 zones in order. First player to complete the circuit wins.
* **sourceManual:** "VolleyCoach Core Library"

### Drill Entry 20: Setter Read the Middle
* **Title:** Setter Vision: Read the Middle
* **Category:** Setting
* **Tags:** ["Setters", "Decision Making", "Vision", "Tactical"]
* **durationMinutes:** 15
* **minPlayers:** 4
* **maxPlayers:** 6
* **Purpose:** * Train setters to use peripheral vision to locate the opposing Middle Blocker before setting.
    * Improve decision-making to isolate hitters against a single block.
* **Instructions:**
    1. Setter is in position. A Tosser is in middle back. A designated "Opposing Middle Blocker" stands on a box on the other side of the net.
    2. Two attackers are ready (Outside Hitter and Right Side).
    3. Tosser feeds a good pass to the setter.
    4. Just before the setter contacts the ball, the "Opposing Middle Blocker" shifts their arms clearly to the left or to the right.
    5. The Setter must read this movement and set the ball in the *opposite* direction to give their hitter a one-on-one.
* **sourceManual:** "VolleyCoach Core Library"

### Drill Entry 21: Libero Wall Rebounds
* **Title:** Libero Wall Rebounds
* **Category:** Defense
* **Tags:** ["Liberos", "Reaction Time", "Solo Drill", "Ball Control"]
* **durationMinutes:** 10
* **minPlayers:** 1
* **maxPlayers:** 4
* **Purpose:** * Increase reaction speed and hand-eye coordination for defensive specialists.
    * Perfect platform angles on hard, unpredictable ricochets.
* **Instructions:**
    1. Player stands 5-7 feet away from a smooth, hard wall.
    2. Player throws the ball hard against the wall or the floor-to-wall corner.
    3. The ball rebounds rapidly; the player must react, stay low, and pass the ball perfectly back to themselves or a designated target spot.
    4. Progression: Have a partner stand behind the player and throw the ball against the wall so the player cannot anticipate the trajectory.
* **sourceManual:** "VolleyCoach Core Library"

### Drill Entry 22: Pass-to-Attack Wash
* **Title:** Pass-to-Attack Wash Drill
* **Category:** Serve/Receive
* **Tags:** ["Wash Drill", "Passing", "Transition", "6v6"]
* **durationMinutes:** 20
* **minPlayers:** 12
* **maxPlayers:** 14
* **Purpose:** * Reward excellent serve-receive by requiring a first-ball sideout to score.
    * Punish poor passing by forcing teams to defend out-of-system.
* **Instructions:**
    1. 6v6 setup. Team A is Receiving, Team B is Serving.
    2. Team B serves the ball. Team A must pass, set, and get a kill on the *very first swing* to earn a "Big Point".
    3. If Team A fails to kill the first ball (it's dug, blocked, or an error), the rally continues, but the point is only worth a "Small Point" (half a point) for whichever team eventually wins the rally.
    4. Play to 10 Big Points, then swap Serving and Receiving teams.
* **sourceManual:** "VolleyCoach Core Library"

### Drill Entry 23: The 'Z' Drill
* **Title:** The 'Z' Drill (Agility & Digging)
* **Category:** Conditioning
* **Tags:** ["Agility", "Defense", "Footwork", "High Intensity"]
* **durationMinutes:** 10
* **minPlayers:** 1
* **maxPlayers:** 12
* **Purpose:** * Combine high-intensity cardiovascular conditioning with defensive posture and floor skills (sprawls/dives).
* **Instructions:**
    1. Set up 4 cones in a large 'Z' shape taking up half the court.
    2. Player starts at Cone 1 (bottom left).
    3. Sprint forward to Cone 2 (top left).
    4. Coach hits a ball just out of reach; Player executes a defensive sprawl/dive to dig it.
    5. Player pops up immediately, defensive shuffles diagonally to Cone 3 (bottom right).
    6. Coach hits another ball; Player executes a second dive.
    7. Player pops up, sprints forward to Cone 4 (top right).
* **sourceManual:** "VolleyCoach Core Library"

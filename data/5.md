### Drill Entry 24: 4-on-4 Plus 1 (Transition)
* **Title:** 4-on-4 Plus 1
* **Category:** Gameplay
* **Tags:** ["Small Sided", "Transition", "Out of System", "Communication"]
* **durationMinutes:** 20
* **minPlayers:** 10
* **maxPlayers:** 15
* **Purpose:** * Train players to communicate and organize rapidly in chaotic, out-of-system scenarios.
    * Emphasize back-row attacking and defensive coverage.
* **Instructions:**
    1. Set up two teams of 4 players on the court (typically a Setter, Middle, Outside, and Libero). 
    2. Place 1 additional player ("Plus 1") for each team off the court near the baseline.
    3. Coach slaps a ball and initiates a free ball to Team A.
    4. The moment the ball crosses the net, the "Plus 1" player must sprint onto the court and integrate into the offense/defense.
    5. Play the rally out. The team that loses the point rotates their "Plus 1" player with someone on the court.
* **sourceManual:** "VolleyCoach Core Library"

### Drill Entry 25: Read and React Blocking
* **Title:** Read and React Blocking
* **Category:** Defense
* **Tags:** ["Blocking", "Middles", "Pins", "Vision"]
* **durationMinutes:** 15
* **minPlayers:** 4
* **maxPlayers:** 8
* **Purpose:** * Train Middle Blockers to read the setter's hands and the hitter's approach before committing.
    * Improve timing and forming a solid double block.
* **Instructions:**
    1. Set up a full front row (Outside, Middle, Right Side) in blocking base on one side.
    2. On the opposite side, place a Setter at the net and Tossers in the backcourt.
    3. Tosser feeds the Setter. The Setter randomly sets the pins or the middle.
    4. The defending Middle Blocker must hold their ground, read the set, and execute explosive crossover footwork to close the block with the respective pin player.
    5. Attackers take controlled swings into the block.
* **sourceManual:** "VolleyCoach Core Library"

### Drill Entry 26: The "21" Passing Game
* **Title:** The "21" Passing Game
* **Category:** Serve/Receive
* **Tags:** ["Serve Receive", "Pressure", "Scoring"]
* **durationMinutes:** 15
* **minPlayers:** 6
* **maxPlayers:** 14
* **Purpose:** * Put primary passers under intense pressure to deliver perfect 3-point passes.
    * Simulate the pressure of passing at the end of a tight set.
* **Instructions:**
    1. Put 3 passers in Serve Receive formation. Place a target box or catcher at the Setter position.
    2. The rest of the team serves aggressively from the opposite baseline.
    3. Scoring: A perfect pass (3-pass) earns +1 point. A medium pass (2-pass) earns 0 points. A poor pass, overpass, or ace (1 or 0-pass) costs -1 point.
    4. The receiving trio must reach 21 points to "win" and rotate out. If they drop below -5, they are penalized with a sprint and reset to 0.
* **sourceManual:** "VolleyCoach Core Library"

### Drill Entry 27: Tooling the Block
* **Title:** Attacking: Tooling the Block
* **Category:** Attack
* **Tags:** ["Hitting", "Vision", "Tooling", "Smart Play"]
* **durationMinutes:** 10
* **minPlayers:** 4
* **maxPlayers:** 10
* **Purpose:** * Teach attackers to use the opposing block to their advantage when the set is tight or the block is massive.
    * Develop the "wipe" and high-hands techniques.
* **Instructions:**
    1. Place two designated blockers on boxes on the defensive side of the net, holding blocking pads or just their arms stiff over the net.
    2. A Setter and a line of Attackers are on the offensive side.
    3. Setter pushes balls to the Attackers.
    4. Attackers must purposefully aim for the outside hand of the blocker to "wipe" the ball out of bounds, or aim high off the fingertips to send the ball deep out of bounds.
* **sourceManual:** "VolleyCoach Core Library"

### Drill Entry 28: Dig or Die (Continuous Defense)
* **Title:** Dig or Die
* **Category:** Defense
* **Tags:** ["Digging", "Grit", "High Intensity", "Floor Work"]
* **durationMinutes:** 10
* **minPlayers:** 2
* **maxPlayers:** 8
* **Purpose:** * Build defensive tenacity and a "never let the ball hit the floor" mentality.
    * Improve recovery time after a sprawl or roll.
* **Instructions:**
    1. One defender starts in middle back (Zone 6). The Coach stands on a box at the center of the net with a full cart of balls.
    2. The Coach rapidly hits, tips, and throws balls to all corners of the court.
    3. The defender must touch every single ball, keeping it off the floor, regardless of how chaotic it gets.
    4. The drill continues for exactly 60 seconds per player. The player must get back to their feet as fast as possible after every dive.
* **sourceManual:** "VolleyCoach Core Library"

### Drill Entry 29: Setter Chase & Set
* **Title:** Setter Chase & Set
* **Category:** Setting
* **Tags:** ["Setters", "Conditioning", "Out of System", "Footwork"]
* **durationMinutes:** 12
* **minPlayers:** 2
* **maxPlayers:** 4
* **Purpose:** * Train setters to track down poor passes and deliver a hittable high ball to the pins.
    * Build setter specific cardiovascular stamina.
* **Instructions:**
    1. Setter starts in base (Zone 2). A target catcher is at the left pin (Zone 4).
    2. The Coach stands near the baseline with a cart of balls.
    3. The Coach purposefully tosses balls far out of system—deep into the corners, tight to the net, or off the court.
    4. The Setter must sprint to the ball, square up to the target (Zone 4), and deliver a high, hittable set.
    5. Setters perform 10 consecutive chaotic sets before rotating.
* **sourceManual:** "VolleyCoach Core Library"

### Drill Entry 30: Butterfly Sprints & Sprawls
* **Title:** Butterfly Sprints & Sprawls
* **Category:** Conditioning
* **Tags:** ["Cardio", "Floor Work", "Agility"]
* **durationMinutes:** 8
* **minPlayers:** 1
* **maxPlayers:** 15
* **Purpose:** * Combine line-touch conditioning with volleyball-specific floor movements.
* **Instructions:**
    1. Players line up on the baseline.
    2. Sprint to the 3-meter (10-foot) line, execute a forward sprawl (chest to floor), pop up quickly.
    3. Backpedal to the baseline.
    4. Sprint to the center line (under the net), execute a sprawl, pop up.
    5. Backpedal to the baseline.
    6. Complete 3 full sets with a 30-second rest in between.
* **sourceManual:** "VolleyCoach Core Library"

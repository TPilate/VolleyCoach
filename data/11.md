### Drill Entry 70: Warriors and Dragons
* **Title:** Warriors and Dragons (Movement)
* **Category:** Warm-up
* **Tags:** ["Youth", "Primary", "Agility", "Game-Based"]
* **durationMinutes:** 10
* **minPlayers:** 8
* **maxPlayers:** 20
* **Purpose:** * Develop quick changes of direction and spatial awareness in a fun, high-energy environment.
* **Instructions:**
    1. Define a "safe zone" at each end of the court.
    2. Two "Dragons" stand in the middle; the rest are "Warriors" at one end.
    3. Warriors must run to the other safe zone without being tagged by a Dragon.
    4. If tagged, a Warrior becomes a "Stone" (must crouch). They can be revived if another Warrior high-fives them.
* **sourceManual:** "Volleyball Lesson Plan FA-1"

### Drill Entry 71: The "X" Combination Attack
* **Title:** The "X" Play (Cross-Pattern)
* **Category:** Attack
* **Tags:** ["Elite", "Tactical", "Combinations", "Middles"]
* **durationMinutes:** 20
* **minPlayers:** 6
* **maxPlayers:** 10
* **Purpose:** * Overload the opposing middle blocker by crossing two attackers in the same zone.
* **Instructions:**
    1. Middle Attacker (M) approaches for a "Quick 1" set.
    2. The Right-Side Attacker (RS) sprints behind the Middle to hit a "2" set in the same gap.
    3. Setter must hide the ball until the last moment to confuse the block.
* **sourceManual:** "Train to Train Matrix (15/16)"

### Drill Entry 72: Double Block Closure
* **Title:** Double Block: Closing the Seam
* **Category:** Defense
* **Tags:** ["Blocking", "Teamwork", "Advanced"]
* **durationMinutes:** 15
* **minPlayers:** 5
* **maxPlayers:** 8
* **Purpose:** * Ensure the pin blocker and middle blocker "seal" the space between them so no ball can pass through.
* **Instructions:**
    1. A pin blocker sets the "Wall" on the sideline.
    2. The middle blocker executes a crossover step to close the gap.
    3. Both jumpers must touch shoulders in the air to ensure the "seam" is closed.
    4. Coach hits balls specifically at the gap between the two blockers.
* **sourceManual:** "USA Volleyball Training Diagrams"

### Drill Entry 73: 2-v-2 Continuous Tennis
* **Title:** Continuous Tennis (Ball Control)
* **Category:** Gameplay
* **Tags:** ["Youth", "Control", "Conditioning"]
* **durationMinutes:** 15
* **minPlayers:** 4
* **maxPlayers:** 12
* **Purpose:** * Improve long-rally stamina and the "free ball" return quality.
* **Instructions:**
    1. Players play 2-v-2 on a narrow court.
    2. The ball must be sent over the net using a forearm pass or overhead pass on the first or second contact.
    3. If the ball hits the floor, the team that let it drop is replaced immediately by a new duo.
* **sourceManual:** "Volleyball Lesson Plan FA-1"

### Drill Entry 74: Serve-Receive "Cue Reading"
* **Title:** Reading the Server's Intent
* **Category:** Serve/Receive
* **Tags:** ["Tactical", "Vision", "Mental"]
* **durationMinutes:** 12
* **minPlayers:** 4
* **maxPlayers:** 10
* **Purpose:** * Train passers to identify where a serve is going before the ball even crosses the net.
* **Instructions:**
    1. Passers watch the server's toss and arm swing.
    2. The moment the server contacts the ball, passers must shout "Deep," "Short," "Left," or "Right."
    3. Focus on the server's hand contact point and shoulder orientation as the primary cues.
* **sourceManual:** "Learn to Compete Matrix (17/18)"

### Drill Entry 75: Middle Blocker "Stack" Defense
* **Title:** Stack Blocking (Zone Defense)
* **Category:** Defense
* **Tags:** ["Blocking", "Systems", "Tactical"]
* **durationMinutes:** 15
* **minPlayers:** 6
* **maxPlayers:** 9
* **Purpose:** * Position two blockers in one zone to shut down a dominant opponent hitter.
* **Instructions:**
    1. Two blockers start side-by-side in Zone 3 (Middle).
    2. As the opponent setter releases the ball to the "hot" hitter, both blockers move in tandem to that zone.
    3. This "stacks" the defense against the opponent's primary offensive weapon.
* **sourceManual:** "USA Volleyball Training Diagrams"

### Drill Entry 10: Setter Box Footwork
* **Title:** Setter Box Footwork & Location
* **Category:** Setting
* **Tags:** ["Footwork", "Setters", "Precision", "Repetition"]
* **durationMinutes:** 10
* **minPlayers:** 2
* **maxPlayers:** 4
* **Purpose:** * Perfect the setter's footwork from target position to the ball.
    * Train setters to square their shoulders to the left pin regardless of where the pass pulls them.
* **Instructions:**
    1. Place a target box or catcher at the Outside Hitter position (Zone 4). 
    2. The Coach stands in the middle back (Zone 6) with a cart of balls.
    3. The Coach slaps the ball to initiate the setter's movement from base (Zone 2) to the target area (Zone 2.5).
    4. The Coach tosses the ball to various locations (perfect, off the net, pushed towards zone 4, pushed towards zone 2).
    5. The Setter must use proper footwork (Left-Right for right-handed setters) to get under the ball, square to the box, and set.
* **sourceManual:** "VolleyCoach Core Library"

### Drill Entry 11: Dead Man Serving
* **Title:** Dead Man Serving (Target Practice)
* **Category:** Serve/Receive
* **Tags:** ["Serving", "Accuracy", "Pressure"]
* **durationMinutes:** 10
* **minPlayers:** 6
* **maxPlayers:** 14
* **Purpose:** * Force servers to hit specific zones on the court under pressure.
    * Provide immediate visual feedback on serving accuracy.
* **Instructions:**
    1. Divide the team in half. Team A serves, Team B acts as the "Dead Men".
    2. Team B players lie flat on their backs in specific zones on the receiving side (e.g., deep corners, short zones).
    3. Team A servers must aim and serve the ball. 
    4. If a server hits a "Dead Man" or lands the ball within an arm's reach of them, the Dead Man is "revived" and joins the serving side.
    5. Play until all Dead Men are revived, or set a time limit and switch sides.
* **sourceManual:** "VolleyCoach Core Library"

### Drill Entry 12: 3-on-3 Back Row Attack (Bic/Pipe)
* **Title:** 3-on-3 Back Row Attack
* **Category:** Attack
* **Tags:** ["Back Row", "Bic", "Pipe", "3v3"]
* **durationMinutes:** 15
* **minPlayers:** 6
* **maxPlayers:** 12
* **Purpose:** * Develop aggressive back-row attacking options (Pipe and Bic).
    * Train defenders to read back-row attackers.
* **Instructions:**
    1. Create two teams of 3. All players must remain behind the 3-meter (10-foot) line at all times.
    2. The Coach enters a free ball to Team A.
    3. Team A must pass, set, and attack the ball from behind the 10-foot line.
    4. Front-row blocking is not allowed; this is purely a digging and attacking drill.
    5. Emphasize the setter pushing the ball fast to the center-back (Pipe) or fast to the back-left/right gaps.
* **sourceManual:** "VolleyCoach Core Library"

### Drill Entry 13: Coach on a Box - Rapid Digging
* **Title:** Rapid Fire Digging (Coach on Box)
* **Category:** Defense
* **Tags:** ["Digging", "Reaction Time", "High Intensity"]
* **durationMinutes:** 12
* **minPlayers:** 3
* **maxPlayers:** 9
* **Purpose:** * Improve reaction time and digging posture against hard-driven attacks.
    * Teach defenders to hold their ground and absorb the impact of the ball.
* **Instructions:**
    1. The Coach stands on a sturdy box on the attacker's side of the net (Zone 4 or Zone 2).
    2. 3 Defenders line up on the opposite side in base positions (Zones 5, 6, 1).
    3. The Coach rapidly hits down-balls and hard-driven spikes at the defenders.
    4. Defenders must dig the ball high to the middle of the court.
    5. Rotate a new trio of defenders in after 10 consecutive digs.
* **sourceManual:** "VolleyCoach Core Library"

### Drill Entry 14: 6v6 Wash Drill (Free/Down/Serve)
* **Title:** 6v6 Wash Drill (3-Ball Sequence)
* **Category:** Gameplay
* **Tags:** ["6v6", "Wash Drill", "Competitive", "Transition"]
* **durationMinutes:** 25
* **minPlayers:** 12
* **maxPlayers:** 14
* **Purpose:** * Simulate real match pressure and train teams to sustain focus over long rallies.
    * Practice scoring in three different phases of the game.
* **Instructions:**
    1. Set up two 6-player teams. A "Wash" consists of 3 distinct mini-games.
    2. Ball 1: Coach tosses a Free Ball to Team A. They must score.
    3. Ball 2: Coach hits a hard Down-Ball to Team B. They must score.
    4. Ball 3: A designated server from either team serves a live ball.
    5. A team must win 2 out of the 3 balls to win the "Wash" and earn 1 point on the main scoreboard. If they don't, the score remains 0-0. Play to 5.
* **sourceManual:** "VolleyCoach Core Library"

### Drill Entry 15: Out of System Setting (Non-Setters)
* **Title:** Libero/OH Out of System Setting
* **Category:** Setting
* **Tags:** ["Out of System", "Libero", "Bump Set"]
* **durationMinutes:** 10
* **minPlayers:** 4
* **maxPlayers:** 12
* **Purpose:** * Train non-setters (Liberos, Outside Hitters) to deliver a hittable ball when the setter plays the first contact.
    * Improve bump-setting accuracy to the pins.
* **Instructions:**
    1. Form a line of players (Liberos and Hitters) in middle back (Zone 6).
    2. Coach stands in the setter position (Zone 2) and deliberately passes the ball high into the middle of the court, simulating a dug ball by the setter.
    3. The player in Zone 6 must step in, call "Help!", and bump-set the ball high and 3 feet off the net to the Outside Hitter (Zone 4).
    4. An attacker takes a swing, and players rotate.
* **sourceManual:** "VolleyCoach Core Library"

### Drill Entry 16: Figure 8 Footwork & Pass
* **Title:** Figure 8 Passing
* **Category:** Serve/Receive
* **Tags:** ["Footwork", "Passing", "Warm-up"]
* **durationMinutes:** 8
* **minPlayers:** 3
* **maxPlayers:** 6
* **Purpose:** * Warm up passing posture and lateral footwork.
    * Ensure players are moving their feet to get behind the ball rather than reaching.
* **Instructions:**
    1. Place two cones about 8 feet apart in the backcourt.
    2. A passer starts in the middle of the cones. The Coach/Tosser stands at the net.
    3. The passer shuffles in a Figure-8 pattern around the cones.
    4. As the passer crosses the center point, the Coach tosses a ball.
    5. The passer must stop, establish a stable platform, pass the ball back to the Coach, and immediately resume the Figure-8 footwork.
    6. Work for 45-second intervals.
* **sourceManual:** "VolleyCoach Core Library"

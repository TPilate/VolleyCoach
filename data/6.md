### Drill Entry 31: Server vs. Passer Isolation
* **Title:** Server vs. Passer Isolation
* **Category:** Serve/Receive
* **Tags:** ["Serving", "Passing", "1-on-1", "Focus"]
* **durationMinutes:** 10
* **minPlayers:** 2
* **maxPlayers:** 8
* **Purpose:** * Create a high-pressure, 1-on-1 battle between a server and a passer.
    * Train passers to hold their platform and read the server's arm swing without the distraction of adjacent teammates.
* **Instructions:**
    1. Place one passer in middle back (Zone 6). Place a target catcher in the Setter position.
    2. Place one server on the opposite baseline. 
    3. The server serves directly at the passer. 
    4. Scoring: Server gets 1 point for an ace or a 0/1-pass. Passer gets 1 point for a perfect 3-pass.
    5. First player to 5 points wins the round, then swap out players.
* **sourceManual:** "VolleyCoach Core Library"

### Drill Entry 32: Jump Serve Toss Mechanics
* **Title:** Jump Serve Toss & Catch
* **Category:** Warm-up
* **Tags:** ["Serving", "Jump Serve", "Mechanics", "Solo Drill"]
* **durationMinutes:** 5
* **minPlayers:** 1
* **maxPlayers:** 14
* **Purpose:** * Isolate and perfect the most critical part of the jump serve: the toss.
    * Develop consistent height, spin (or float), and forward trajectory.
* **Instructions:**
    1. Players line up on the baseline, each with a ball.
    2. Players execute their standard jump serve approach (3 or 4 steps) and toss the ball.
    3. Instead of hitting the ball, the player must jump and let the ball drop. 
    4. The ball should ideally land just inside the baseline, slightly in front of their hitting shoulder.
    5. Players perform 15 consecutive tosses, focusing purely on consistent landing spots and height.
* **sourceManual:** "VolleyCoach Core Library"

### Drill Entry 33: Out-of-System High Ball Setting
* **Title:** Out-of-System High Ball Setting
* **Category:** Setting
* **Tags:** ["Setting", "Out of System", "Pins"]
* **durationMinutes:** 12
* **minPlayers:** 4
* **maxPlayers:** 10
* **Purpose:** * Train all players (not just setters) to deliver a high, hittable ball to the pins from deep in the court.
    * Emphasize putting air under the ball to give the attacker time to approach.
* **Instructions:**
    1. Coach stands at the net and tosses a high, deep ball to Zone 6 or Zone 5.
    2. A designated setter (or non-setter) sprints to the ball.
    3. The player must set the ball high to the Outside Hitter (Zone 4) from 15+ feet away.
    4. The attacker takes a swing.
    5. Switch targets to the Right Side (Zone 2) after 5 minutes.
* **sourceManual:** "VolleyCoach Core Library"

### Drill Entry 34: 5-on-5 No Middle Scrimmage
* **Title:** 5-on-5 No Middle Scrimmage
* **Category:** Gameplay
* **Tags:** ["Small Sided", "Defense", "Pin Attacking"]
* **durationMinutes:** 20
* **minPlayers:** 10
* **maxPlayers:** 10
* **Purpose:** * Force teams to rely heavily on pin attackers and back-row defense.
    * Create massive gaps in the block to test back-row defensive reads.
* **Instructions:**
    1. Set up two teams of 5 players. Remove the Middle Blocker/Middle Attacker position from both sides.
    2. Teams play out points normally, but all sets must go to the Outside Hitter, Right Side, or Back Row.
    3. Without a middle block, defenders must adapt their positioning to dig hard cross-court and line shots.
    4. Play to 15 points.
* **sourceManual:** "VolleyCoach Core Library"

### Drill Entry 35: Hitter Coverage (The "Shadow" Drill)
* **Title:** Hitter Coverage (Shadow Drill)
* **Category:** Defense
* **Tags:** ["Coverage", "Attacking", "Team Defense"]
* **durationMinutes:** 10
* **minPlayers:** 6
* **maxPlayers:** 12
* **Purpose:** * Train the team to automatically collapse and surround the attacker to cover blocked balls.
    * Build the instinct to stay low and react to ricochets.
* **Instructions:**
    1. Set up a standard 6-person offense. 
    2. Place two designated blockers on boxes on the defensive side of the net, holding blocking pads.
    3. Coach tosses a free ball to the offensive team.
    4. The offensive team passes, sets, and attacks. As the attacker goes up, the rest of the team must "shadow" them, dropping low into a coverage cup around the hitter.
    5. The attacker purposefully hits directly into the blocking pads, and the covering players must dig the rebound to keep the rally alive.
* **sourceManual:** "VolleyCoach Core Library"

### Drill Entry 36: The "Star" Conditioning Drill
* **Title:** The Star Sprint Drill
* **Category:** Conditioning
* **Tags:** ["Cardio", "Agility", "Footwork"]
* **durationMinutes:** 8
* **minPlayers:** 1
* **maxPlayers:** 14
* **Purpose:** * Build explosive multi-directional speed and change-of-direction mechanics.
* **Instructions:**
    1. Place a cone in the exact center of the court. Place 5 cones evenly spaced around it in a large circle (creating a star pattern).
    2. Player starts at the center cone.
    3. On the whistle, the player sprints to Cone 1, touches it, and sprints back to the center.
    4. Immediately sprint to Cone 2, touch, and return to the center.
    5. Repeat for all 5 cones. 
    6. Run 4 sets per player with a 45-second rest between sets.
* **sourceManual:** "VolleyCoach Core Library"

### Drill Entry 37: 3-Man Continuous Digging
* **Title:** 3-Man Continuous Digging
* **Category:** Defense
* **Tags:** ["Digging", "High Reps", "Endurance"]
* **durationMinutes:** 12
* **minPlayers:** 4
* **maxPlayers:** 12
* **Purpose:** * Build defensive stamina and encourage constant communication.
    * Ensure players reset their feet quickly between contacts.
* **Instructions:**
    1. Three defenders start in the back row (Zones 5, 6, 1).
    2. The Coach stands across the net with a cart of balls.
    3. The Coach rapidly attacks the ball over the net, alternating targets.
    4. Defenders must dig the ball up to the setter target area.
    5. The moment a defender digs, they must touch the baseline behind them before re-entering the play.
    6. The trio must successfully dig 15 balls to the target before rotating out.
* **sourceManual:** "VolleyCoach Core Library"

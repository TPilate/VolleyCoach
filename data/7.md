### Drill Entry 45: Perimeter Defense - Transition to Attack
* **Title:** Perimeter Defense: Read and Transition
* **Category:** Defense
* **Tags:** ["Systems", "Perimeter", "Transition", "Advanced"]
* **durationMinutes:** 20
* **minPlayers:** 6
* **maxPlayers:** 12
* **Purpose:** * Train defenders to start on the court boundary and move inward based on the hitter's shoulder.
    * Improve the transition speed from a dig to an available attacking approach.
* **Instructions:**
    1. Set up 6 defenders in a "Perimeter" system (deep on lines and corners).
    2. Coach initiates a hit from a box at the net.
    3. Defenders move from the perimeter to the ball, dig to the setter.
    4. All non-digging players must immediately transition outside the court to prepare for a counter-attack.
    5. Play continues until the ball is "killed" or out of bounds.
* **sourceManual:** "Defensive Systems Manual"

### Drill Entry 46: Multiple-Toss Hitting (High Reps)
* **Title:** Multiple-Toss Hitting
* **Category:** Attack
* **Tags:** ["Hitting", "Conditioning", "Adjustment", "Repetition"]
* **durationMinutes:** 10
* **minPlayers:** 2
* **maxPlayers:** 8
* **Purpose:** * Teach hitters to adjust to sets of varying heights and speeds.
    * Build the "reset" habit—transitioning off the net immediately after a swing.
* **Instructions:**
    1. Coach stands at the net with a cart of 10 balls. Attacker starts at the 3m line.
    2. Coach tosses balls at irregular intervals with varying heights.
    3. Attacker hits, lands, and must retreat as far off the net as possible before the next toss.
    4. Goal: Complete all 10 hits with explosive approaches.
* **sourceManual:** "Mary Wise: Drills for Champions"

### Drill Entry 47: Line vs. Crosscourt Competition
* **Title:** Line/Crosscourt Attack Competition
* **Category:** Attack
* **Tags:** ["Accuracy", "Competitive", "Shot Making"]
* **durationMinutes:** 15
* **minPlayers:** 4
* **maxPlayers:** 12
* **Purpose:** * Improve an attacker's ability to "see" the court and hit around a block.
    * Reward precision over raw power.
* **Instructions:**
    1. Mark two specific zones on the court: a narrow "Line" alley and a deep "Crosscourt" corner.
    2. Setter sets the attacker. Just before contact, the Coach shouts "Line!" or "Cross!".
    3. Attacker must adjust their wrist and arm swing to hit the designated zone.
    4. Scoring: +1 point for hitting the correct zone, -1 for hitting the wrong one or out.
* **sourceManual:** "Mary Wise: Drills for Champions"

### Drill Entry 48: Rover (Man-Up) Defense
* **Title:** Rover / Man-Up System Drill
* **Category:** Defense
* **Tags:** ["Systems", "Tactical", "Tips", "Middle Back"]
* **durationMinutes:** 15
* **minPlayers:** 6
* **maxPlayers:** 12
* **Purpose:** * Train the Middle Back (MB) to play "Rover"—coming up short to cover tips and off-speed shots.
    * Optimize defense against teams that drop a lot of "junk" balls in the middle of the court.
* **Instructions:**
    1. Defensive 6-man setup with the MB starting 5 feet in front of the baseline.
    2. Opposing attackers alternate between hard swings and soft tips/rolls.
    3. The "Rover" (MB) is responsible for any ball that drops in the "doughnut" (center of the court).
    4. Back-row corners stay deep for the hard-driven line shots.
* **sourceManual:** "Defensive Systems Manual"

### Drill Entry 49: Parallel Movement Digging
* **Title:** Parallel Movement Series
* **Category:** Defense
* **Tags:** ["Footwork", "Digging", "Chemistry"]
* **durationMinutes:** 12
* **minPlayers:** 3
* **maxPlayers:** 6
* **Purpose:** * Teach the back-row trio to move in unison (parallel) relative to the ball's movement on the other side.
    * Ensure no "holes" open up in the defense during setter movement.
* **Instructions:**
    1. Three back-row defenders in base position. 
    2. Coach moves across the net with the ball (Left to Right). 
    3. Defenders must shuffle in unison, maintaining their relative spacing.
    4. On a whistle, Coach hits a ball; defenders must stop and dig from their new position.
* **sourceManual:** "Defensive Systems Manual"

### Drill Entry 50: 3-Man "SSP" (Serve-Pass-Spike)
* **Title:** SSP (Serve-Pass-Spike) Rhythm
* **Category:** Gameplay
* **Tags:** ["Rhythm", "Cooperation", "Beginner-Intermediate"]
* **durationMinutes:** 15
* **minPlayers:** 4
* **maxPlayers:** 12
* **Purpose:** * Connect the three most important phases of a volleyball rally.
    * Establish a flow from serve-receive to offensive finishing.
* **Instructions:**
    1. 3 Passers on one side, 1 Server on the other.
    2. Server serves. Passers must pass to the target.
    3. The passer who touched the ball must then immediately transition to the net to become the attacker.
    4. Setter (Coach or Player) sets the passer for a spike.
    5. Rotate after 3 successful cycles.
* **sourceManual:** "Muni 8-Week Training"

### Drill Entry 51: TRX Suspended Plank & Knee-In
* **Title:** TRX Core Stability (Volleyball Specific)
* **Category:** Conditioning
* **Tags:** ["TRX", "Core", "Stability", "Strength"]
* **durationMinutes:** 8
* **minPlayers:** 1
* **maxPlayers:** 10
* **Purpose:** * Build the core strength required for stable platform passing and mid-air attacking adjustments.
* **Instructions:**
    1. Place feet in TRX foot cradles under the anchor point.
    2. Assume a forearm plank position. 
    3. Hold for 30 seconds.
    4. Progression: Pull both knees into the chest while keeping the back flat, then extend back to plank.
    5. Perform 3 sets of 12 reps.
* **sourceManual:** "CWU Wildcats Training Book"
